# Senior Manager – Updated Core Role

They are responsible for:

- **Approvals** (risk control)
- **Stock intake** (receiving deliveries)
- **Stock adjustments**
- **Discrepancies**
- **Promotions**
- **Operational reporting**

They control **inventory + floor risk**.

---

## Stock Intake – Proper Process Flow (Premium Setup)

This must be structured — no shortcuts.

### Step 1 – Delivery Arrives

Supplier delivers stock.

**Senior Manager:**

1. Opens **Stock → Receive Delivery**
2. Selects **supplier**
3. Enters **invoice number**
4. Enters **delivery reference**

### Step 2 – Capture Expected Items

They record:

- Item name
- Quantity delivered
- Cost price (optional but recommended)
- Batch/lot number (optional)
- Delivery date

**System does NOT increase stock yet.**

### Step 3 – Physical Verification

Senior Manager must:

1. Physically count items
2. Compare with invoice
3. Confirm quantities

**If mismatch:**

- System flags **discrepancy**
- Requires reason:
  - Supplier short delivery
  - Damaged goods
  - Counting error

### Step 4 – Confirm Intake

After verification:

Senior Manager clicks: **“Confirm Stock Intake”**

**System then:**

- Increases stock levels
- Logs intake
- Links it to:
  - Supplier
  - Invoice number
  - Manager ID
  - Date & time

This creates a **permanent audit trail**.

---

## Important Rule (Premium Control)

**Senior Manager:**

- Can receive stock
- Can record delivery
- Can adjust stock with reason

**But:**

- Cannot delete stock intake history
- Cannot edit old confirmed deliveries
- Cannot backdate without audit log

**Only Owner** can reverse or audit.
